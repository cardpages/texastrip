Arrival in Christiansburg: ~9:15 PM

🚗 8:15 AM — Depart Hackensack, NJ   3 hr 15 min to Carlisle

  • Enjoy breakfast at the condo before hitting the road.
  • Take interstates toward Harrisburg/Carlisle for speed and efficiency early in the day.

🍽️ 11:45 AM — Lunch at Momma Spriggs, Carlisle, PA

  • Fresh, homestyle food with friendly service. Local favorite; outdoor seating available.
  • ⏰ ~1 hour

🚗 1:00 PM — Depart Carlisle, PA   ~2 hr to Front Royal Entrance Station

  • Hop back on I‑81 S to I‑66 E.
  • Take US‑340 S / US‑522 S toward Front Royal / Skyline Drive.
  • Maybe stop in Front Royal for snacks/drinks — Skyline Drive has limited food options.

⭐ 3:00 PM — Enter Skyline Drive at Front Royal Entrance

Begin a short, scenic drive through Shenandoah National Park — about 35 miles south to Thornton Gap, with relaxing stops:

  • Dickey Ridge Visitor Center — restrooms, exhibits, overlook (grab a map).
  • Signal Knob Overlook — long views to the west.
  • Hogback Overlook — one of the highest overlooks on Skyline Drive.
  • Elkwallow Wayside — rest stop with snacks and restrooms.
  • Thornton Hollow Overlook — peaceful and often uncrowded.

⏰ Spend about ~2 hours enjoying the drive and the views.

🚗 5:00 PM — Exit Skyline Drive at Thornton Gap and descend into Luray, VA

  • Follow the brown sign toward Washington DC / Luray.
  • Use maps for directions to the restaurant location from the last overlook so you don’t miss the exit.

🍽️ 5:15 PM — Dinner in Luray, VA
