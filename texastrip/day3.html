🚗 Day 3: MONDAY — Nashville, TN → Arkadelphia, AR

Total driving time: ~7 hours (excluding stops)
Departure time: 9:00 AM
Arrival in Arkadelphia: ~9:00 PM
General Route: I‑40 W → I‑30 W

🔗 Google Maps route overview  |  📍 List of Key Places

🚗 9:00 AM — Depart Nashville, TN   1 hr 50 min to Jackson

  • Take advantage of the time change by leaving at 9AM CENTRAL
  • Eat breakfast at the hotel before leaving.
  • Take I‑40 W toward Jackson for speed and simplicity.
  • If you need snacks/drinks: Pilot Travel Center (other stops on this leg may not have shops).

⭐ 11:00 AM — Arrive in Jackson, TN (Casey Jones Village)

  • Start at the Visitor Center.
  • Explore trains, exhibits, and the gift shop.
  • Light walking, shaded areas, benches — ⏰ ~45 minutes
  • OPTIONAL: Early lunch at Old Country Store Restaurant (on‑site, Southern food). If you keep it quick, you’ll still reach Memphis with time for an extra activity.

🚗 11:45 AM — Depart Jackson, TN   50 min to Somerville

  • Take I‑40 W to exit 52. Follow TN‑76 W (southward).

🍽️ 12:35 PM — Lunch at The Hut, Somerville, TN

  • Classic Southern comfort food; good seating and parking; friendly service.
  • Other similar spots in town if needed. — ⏰ ~1 hour

🚗 1:30 PM — Depart Somerville, TN   1 hr 5 min to Memphis

  • Drive west on US‑64 W to I‑40 W.

⭐ 2:30 PM — Arrive near Beale Street, Memphis, TN

  • Park at LAZ Parking – 2nd Street Garage or another nearby garage.
  • Spend 45–90 minutes walking the Beale Street Historic District—bars, shops, landmarks, and statues.
  • Optional: walk a few extra blocks to the Mississippi River near Beale Street Landing.
  • ⏰ ~90 minutes to 2 hours

🚗 4:30 PM — Depart Memphis, TN   2 hr 10 min to Little Rock

  • Drive west on I‑40 W almost the whole way until I‑30 W.
  • If you leave Memphis early and want a slower route, consider US‑64 W, then south to the interstate.

🍽️ 6:40 PM — Dinner at Samantha’s Tap Room & Wood Grill, Little Rock, AR

  • Casual but not too loud or cramped; known for grilled meats, fresh fish, and 32 taps.
  • Cute block to stretch your legs after dinner (good backup restaurants nearby).
  • Optional post‑dinner stroll: Park near Junction Bridge / River Market — flat sidewalks, benches, and riverfront views.
  • ⏰ ~70 to 90 minutes

🚗 8:00 PM — Depart Little Rock, AR   ~1 hr 5 min to hotel

  • Straight shot on I‑30 W to Arkadelphia.

🛏️ 9:00 PM — Check in at hotel

Hampton Inn Arkadelphia
108 Malvern Rd, Arkadelphia, AR 71923

  • Three long days in the books. Tomorrow is mostly driving—home stretch!
