🚗 Day 2: SUNDAY — Christiansburg, VA → Nashville, TN

Total driving time: ~7 hr 45 min (excluding stops)
Departure time: 9:00 AM
Arrival in Nashville: ~9:15 PM (8:15 Central)
General Route: I‑81 S → US‑11W → I‑40 W

🔗 Google Maps route overview  |  📍 List of Key Places

🚗 9:00 AM — Depart Christiansburg, VA   1 hr 35 min to Abingdon

  • Get on the road early for a scenic and comfortable drive down I‑81.
  • Follow I‑81 S to US‑11 S/E Main St in Washington County. Take exit 19 from I‑81 S.

⭐ 10:35 AM — Arrive in Abingdon, VA (Main St / Barter Theatre)

  • Stroll down Main Street.
  • Snap a photo outside the historic Barter Theatre.
  • Optional coffee and restroom stop — ⏰ Time at stop: ~30 minutes

🚗 11:05 AM — Depart Abingdon, VA   1 hr 5 min to Riverfront

🗺️ MAPQUEST FOR THIS LEG — Google driving directions fail

  • Continue on the relaxed and scenic US‑11W S / Lee Hwy toward Kingsport. Some stretches are 11 & 19 (Lee Highway). Stay on this road until Kingsport.

🍽️ 12:00 PM — Lunch at Riverfront Seafood Co., Kingsport, TN

  • Make reservations in advance.
  • Favorite riverside seafood spot with fast, friendly service.
  • Offers diabetic‑friendly and light meal options — ⏰ Time at stop: ~1 hr 15 min

🚗 1:15 PM — Depart Kingsport, TN   1 hr 35 min to Knoxville

  • IF YOU HAVE AN EXTRA 15 MINUTES - Take US‑11W all the way to Knoxville.
  • If you want to hurry, drive southwest toward Knoxville via I‑26 E/US‑23 S → I‑81 S → I‑40 W.

⭐ 2:50 PM — Arrive at Market Square, Knoxville, TN

  • Park in the Market Square Garage (free on weekends).
  • Stroll Market Square and nearby Gay Street.
  • Optional: visit Tennessee Theatre (exterior) or grab a coffee — ⏰ ~1 hour

🚗 4:00 PM — Depart Knoxville, TN   ~3 hours to Nashville

  • Continue west on I‑40 across the Cumberland Plateau all the way to Nashville.

⏰ TIME ZONE CHANGE

  • You will cross into Central time 30 minutes out from Knoxville near Rockwood - keep your eyes peeled for the sign
  • You can catch an extra hour of sleep tonight or stay around downtown Nashville longer.

🍽️ EAT DINNER BETWEEN KNOXVILLE AND NASHVILLE

  • Nashville will be packed so eat up when you get hungry. If you can keep your meal to 30 minutes you can get to Nashville by 6:30pm CENTRAL
