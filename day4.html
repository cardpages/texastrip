🚗 Day 4: TUESDAY - Arkadelphia, AR → San Antonio, TX (via Texarkana, Dallas)

Total driving time: ~8 hrs 30 minutes (excluding stops)
Departure time: 9:00 AM
Arrival in San Antonio: ~9:30 PM
General Route: I-30 W → I-35E S → I-35 S

🔗 Google Maps route overview  |  📍 List of Key Places

🚗 9:00 AM – Depart Arkadelphia, AR   1hr 15min to Texarkana

  • Take I-30 W all the way to Texarkana.

📸 10:15 AM – Arrive in Texarkana, AR (Texas State Line)

  • Plenty of street parking should be available near Texarkana Post Office – State Line Courthouse
  • Snap your "standing in two states" photo and get your 5-15 minutes of rest
    ⏰ Time at stop: ~15 minutes
  • OPTIONAL: Grab a delicious snack at Master Doughnut of Texarkana or Shorty’s Donuts on your way out of town.

🚗 10:30 AM – Depart Texarkana, TX   3 hours to Dallas

  • Take I-30 W the whole way it’s a long drive and late to eat Lunch but that’s what you should do
  • It shouldn’t be too bad stopping near any of the larger towns along this road to make sure you stretch for a few minutes

🍽️ Snack or Small Lunch along the way

  • 12pm - Sulphur Springs - Wendy’s or Metro Diner are on hwy. Whataburger is 5min away
  • 12:30 - Greenville - Wendy’s 1min off hwy & Whataburger 2min
    ⏰ Time at stop: ~25 minutes to eat, rest & stretch

⭐ 1:45 PM – Arrive at DMA Underground Parking Dallas, TX

    Visit the Dallas Museum of Art

  • Cheaper parking on site & Free admission
  • Air-conditioned - Easy stroll through a few galleries
  • Cafe on site to eat if needed
  • Option to walk 1 block to Klyde Warren Park
    • Shaded walking paths
    • Benches, fountains, skyline views
    • Occasional live music and food trucks

⏰ Time at stop: ~1hr 30 minutes OR MORE IF YOU WANT

🚗 3:15 PM – Depart Dallas, TX

5 hours 20 minutes to Home (allow 90 extra mins for stops inc. dinner)

Don’t push too hard. Consider stopping at least 3 times. Make sure you eat. Aim to get home by 9:30pm. I know you want to get home, but don’t burn yourselves out now, it’ll feel worse tomorrow & the next day than it does tonight.

List of Buc-ee’s

  • Hillsboro (1hr from DMA)
  • Temple (2h15m)
  • New Braunfels (4h45m)

🏠🛏️🛏️🛏️🛏️🛏️🛏️ 9:30 PM – Arrive Home
